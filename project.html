TEMPERATURE CONVERTER

A temperature converter is a tool or program that allows you to convert temperatures from one unit to another. The most common temperature units are Celsius (°C), Fahrenheit (°F), and Kelvin (K).

CALCULATOR

A calculator is a device or software application designed to perform mathematical calculations. It's used to perform various arithmetic, scientific, financial, and statistical operations quickly and accurately.

Currency converter

A currency converter is a tool or service that allows you to convert the value of one currency into the equivalent value of another currency.
